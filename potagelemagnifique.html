Potage le Magnifique

Ahhh, the Potage le Magnifique! The signature dish of The Gourmet!

Here, you will be given the full recipe for Potage le Magnifique! Not the partial recipe which causes each cook to make it their own way!

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 1/2 ounces butter
  • 1 wooden flagon of flour
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • 1/2 cup diced onions

Recipe:

  1. Stoke the flames of your open-pit fire, and achieve a low heat.
  2. Combine all ingredients into a large soup pot.
  3. Stir vigorously!
  4. Once hot, pour into earthen soup bowls immediately!
